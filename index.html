About

The Lost Dogs is a new NFT collective, with a mission to build a powerful Web3-native, member-managed community. Individually, lost. Together, strong.


The original idea behind the Lost Dogs comes from us looking at all the existing scattered NFT communities. More often than not, the cooperation and vision of tokenized communities ends at launch. Owners end up as lonely members of a scattered 'community,' with insignificant leadership and development.


We want to change that, and instead build a impactful, long-term members collective. The Lost Dog Collective is designed to attract and house the many community-less NFT owners looking for a proper organization.Â 


Ask yourself: What good could a group of 7,777 members make on the world, both as individuals and also as a collective? We think the possibilities are endless, and we intend to do our best to pioneer the organization towards that mission.Â
